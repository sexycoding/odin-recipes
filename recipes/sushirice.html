It's FISH

WOW... Delicious

Fish, fish, everywhere, but not a drop to... drink? Fish have existed for many years, and yet we are just now discovering their amazing nutritional potential. I remember the days of my mother, taking us all down to the pond next to the ol' radioactive waste treatment plant. We would swim and play in the magical glowing water, while our mother would scoop lazy, tired fish from the pond. She would bake the fish and they had an almost magical taste.

Ingredients

  • Fish, any ol' kind will do
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • A few slices of lemon
  • A sprig of rosemary
